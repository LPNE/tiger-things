Facts



  • Reaching lengths of up to 11 feet, tigers are the largest members of the cat family.



  • Unlike most cats, tigers are good swimmers.



  • Tigers can run at speeds up to around 40mph.



  • A tiger's fur is not only striped, but its skin is striped too. And no two tigers have the same stripe pattern.



  • Tigers are great jumpers. Most can jump up to around 12 feet even from a sitting position.



  • A tiger's roar can be heard from over 2 miles away.
